## Implementation Notes

- Each role has a unique set of menu items tailored to their responsibilities
- All menus include Dashboard and Logout as standard items
- Most roles have access to Account Master for customer management
- Menu items marked "Coming Soon" are placeholders for future features
- The sidebar is dynamically generated based on the user's role
- Icons are carefully selected to match each menu item's purpose
